Logo Creation

The purpose of this project is to create a branding package for "Bagel Bliss," a fictional company. This assignment offers an exciting opportunity to explore creative design and strategic thinking. At the heart of this project is the logo, which serves as the visual cornerstone of the brand identity. Although not explicitly instructed to do so, I was careful to create a vector image that could maintain crisp lines and clarity at any size, whether it's on a business card or a billboard.
